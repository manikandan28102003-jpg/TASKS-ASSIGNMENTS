How to make Biriyani

    Basic Lists

  1. Basmati rice=2cups
  2. Chicken(bone-in or boneless)=500kg
  3. Yogurt=1/2cup
  4. Tomatoes = 1 large
  5. Onion=2 large
  6. Ginger-garlic paste =1
  7. Biryani masala =1 pocket
  8. Red chili powder=1 pocket
  9. Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  10. Ghee or oil
  11. salt
  12. water

Steps for making Biriyani:

1.Wash and soak rice:

basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain before cooking.


2.Marinate the chicken:

In a bowl, mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, lemon juice, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes


3.Cook the chicken:

In the same pot, add whole spices, then saute green chilies and chopped tomatoes. Add the marinated chicken and cook until tender (15–20 minutes).


4.Parboil the rice:

In another pot, boil water with salt and whole spices.


5.Layer the biryani:

In the pot with cooked chicken, spread a layer of partially cooked ric


6.Dum cooking:

Cover the pot tightly (use foil or dough if needed) and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
