It means practicing self-compassion, prioritizing your needs, setting healthy boundaries, and cultivating a positive self-image, which empowers you to handle life's challenges more effectively and build better relationships with others. This journey of self-love is a continuous process of growth and appreciation that is essential for a happy and fulfilling life.
